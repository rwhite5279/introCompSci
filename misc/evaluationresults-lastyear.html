Online Teacher/Course/Text Evaluation Results

In March, 2011, I asked students to complete an online evaluation form for the course. In this form, they indicated how strongly they agreed or disagreed with twenty-three statements, and had the opportunity to submit written comments on four additional questions. Evaluations were performed in-class and submitted anonymously via a web-based evaluation form, in exchange for extra credit points.

Many thanks to my students for their honest feedback on these forms.
